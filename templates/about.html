About Phishing Detector

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to steal sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details. These attacks often come in the form of deceptive emails, messages, or websites that appear trustworthy but are designed to trick victims.

Our Solution

Phishing Detector is an AI-powered web application that helps you identify malicious websites before you fall victim to phishing attacks. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, our system analyzes URLs and extracts over 20 different features to determine the likelihood of a website being malicious.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Detection: Machine learning models trained on thousands of phishing and legitimate URLs
  • Real-Time Analysis: Instant URL scanning with comprehensive feature extraction
  • Detailed Reports: Confidence scores, risk levels, and probability breakdowns
  • Scan History: Track your scans with detailed history and export capabilities
  • VirusTotal Integration: Optional integration with VirusTotal API for enhanced detection
  • Modern UI: Beautiful, responsive interface with dark mode support
